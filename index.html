Enter...ggcov!

ggcov is a Gnome GUI for exploring test coverage data produced by C and C++ programs compiled with gcc --coverage.

Yep

What ggcov does

ggcov tells you which parts of your software are well tested, and which parts...need a little lovin'
All in a way that's convenient for your workflow.
